AMAZON CLOTHING SALES ANALYSIS

ANALYSIS REPORT : 

   
INTRODUCTION

      Dataset Overview:
         Dataset Name:Amazon Sales Report
         Dataset Source: Kaggle

       This exploratory analysis report delves into key insights derived from Amazon sales data, 
       focusing on various aspects such as product sizes, order quantities, shipping methods, product types, buyer demographics, fulfillment channels,
       and geographical distribution within the state of Maharashtra.
      
      1. Product Size Analysis:
       From the bar graph, it is evident that a significant portion of customers prefers purchasing M-Size products. 
       This indicates a potential market trend or consumer preference for medium-sized items.

      2. Order Quantity Distribution:
       The graph illustrates that a majority of customers order products in M-Size, implying that this particular size is popular among consumers,
       leading to higher quantities being sold.
      
       3. Shipping Method Preferences:
       The majority of orders are shipped through courier services, highlighting the reliance on this mode of transportation. 
       This insight could be crucial for logistics and supply chain management optimization.
 
      4. Product Type Preferences:
       The graph indicates that T-shirts are the predominant product type purchased by customers. 
       Understanding popular product categories helps in tailoring marketing strategies and product offerings.

      5. Buyer Demographics:
       Retailers constitute the largest portion of buyers, accounting for 99.3% of the customer base.
       B2B buyers make up a smaller percentage, representing 0.7% of the total buyers. 
       This segmentation is valuable for targeted marketing efforts.
      
       6. Fulfillment Channel Analysis:
        The chart reveals that the majority of sellers choose Amazon as their fulfillment channel. 
        This suggests a high level of trust in Amazon's fulfillment services among sellers.

      7. Geographical Distribution - Maharashtra:
        The graph highlights that a significant proportion of buyers are located in Maharashtra.
        Understanding regional variations can aid in targeted marketing and inventory management strategies for this specific market.

    Recommendations:
 
  - Consider focusing on marketing campaigns and promotions tailored to M-Size products, given their popularity among customers.
  - Optimize logistics and inventory management for courier services, as they are the preferred shipping method for a majority of customers.
  - Emphasize T-shirt offerings in product promotions and marketing strategies to align with customer preferences.
  - Target retailers with specialized promotions and incentives to further capture this dominant buyer segment.
  - Continue to strengthen and promote Amazon as a preferred fulfillment channel for sellers, as indicated by the majority preference.

This exploratory analysis provides valuable insights that can guide strategic decision-making, helping Amazon refine its approach in product offerings,
marketing strategies, logistics, and fulfillment to better meet customer and seller expectations.
