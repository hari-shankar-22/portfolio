NETFLIX DATA VISUALIZATION USING TABLEAU

  
INTRODUCTION

        This Tableau dashboard analysis provides a comprehensive overview of the Netflix dataset,
        focusing on key insights related to the total number of TV shows and movies, 
        their global distribution, yearly production progression from 2008 to 2021, top genre categories, leading directors, 
        and a heatmap illustrating the ratings of TV shows and movies.
    
Dataset Overview:

         Dataset Name:Netflix
         Attributes:Title, Type (TV Show or Movie), Country, Release Year, Genres, Directors, Rating
         Dataset Source: Kaggle
     
Dashboard Components:


       1. Total TV Shows and Movies:
          A visual representation of the total number of TV shows and movies available in the Netflix dataset.
          This provides an overall understanding of the content distribution.

       2. Global Distribution Map:
          A map indicating the countries where most TV shows and movies are produced.
          This geographical representation helps identify regions with significant contributions to Netflix content.

       3. Yearly Progression (2008-2021):
          A line chart showcasing the yearly progression of TV show and movie releases from 2008 to 2021.
          This analysis offers insights into the platform's content growth over the years.

       4. Top 10 Genre Categories:
          A bar chart highlighting the top 10 genre categories for both TV shows and movies.
          This insight aids in understanding the most popular content genres on Netflix.

       5. Top Ten Directors:
        A bar chart presenting the top ten directors based on the number of TV shows and movies directed.
        This helps identify influential creators on the platform.

       6. Heatmap of Ratings:
        A heatmap illustrating the ratings of TV shows and movies. 
        This provides a visual representation of the distribution of ratings, 
        allowing users to identify trends and patterns in audience preferences.


CONCLUSION

The Tableau dashboard analysis of the Netflix dataset offers a holistic view of the platform's content landscape.
Stakeholders can leverage these visualizations to make data-driven decisions, understand audience preferences, and 
identify areas for potential content expansion.
Insights into global content distribution, yearly production trends, popular genres, and 
influential directors provide valuable information for content creators, producers, and Netflix itself.
The heatmap of ratings adds an extra layer of understanding, helping to gauge the reception of different titles.
This Tableau dashboard serves as a powerful tool for exploring and interpreting the Netflix dataset, 
facilitating strategic decision-making in the dynamic and ever-evolving landscape of streaming content.
