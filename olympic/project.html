EXPLORATORY DATA ANALYSIS ON OLYMPIC DATASET

The Olympic Games, a global celebration of athleticism and sportsmanship, have been a prominent event for over a century, providing a platform for athletes from around the world to showcase their talents and compete at the highest level. The wealth of data generated by the Olympic Games presents a unique opportunity for exploratory data analysis (EDA), offering insights into historical trends, athlete performance, and the evolving landscape of sports on a global scale. In this exploration, we delve into a comprehensive Olympic dataset, aiming to uncover patterns, relationships, and noteworthy statistics that have shaped the Games over the years. Our analysis encompasses a wide array of variables, including athlete demographics, medal counts, participating countries, and sport-specific data. By employing statistical methods and graphical representation techniques, we aim to distill meaningful information that not only enriches our understanding of the Olympic Games but also provides a foundation for further in-depth investigations.
